Taekwondo
Origins

Taekwondo is a South Korean martial art. It originated from the intention of creating world peace.

Basics

At its physical foundations, taekwondo is like any other martial art. It consists of defending against attacks and using counterattacks.

Benefits

Taekwondo promotes respect and self-discipline. It encourages a more positive lifestyle through courtesy and self-confidence.

World Taekwondo Headquarters

You can visit the world taekwondo headquarters in South Korea. You can also look up the the International Taekwondo Federation, which is European-based.
